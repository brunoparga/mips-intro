## Content

In
 ordinary English,
**the word
"number" has two different meanings (at least).**
One meaning is the concept,
the other meaning is how the
 concept is represented.
For example,
think about

> "the number of eggs in an egg carton"

You know the number I mean.
Now write it down on  paper.
