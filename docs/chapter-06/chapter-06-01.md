CHAPTER 6 — Number Representation

## Content

Patterns of bits
can represent many different things.
Anything that can be
represented with  symbols of any kind
can be represented with bit patterns.
**This chapter shows how
bit patterns are used to represent integers.**

- Numbers and representations of numbers.
- Positional representation of integers.
- Decimal representation.
- Binary representation.
- Converting representations.
