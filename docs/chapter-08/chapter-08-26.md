Subtraction in Two's Complement

## Answer

Yes. The previous example just did that.

Subtracting an integer X from Y
is the same as adding -X to Y.

## Content

The 
binary addition algorithm is used for subtraction and addition.
**To subtract two numbers represented in two's complement,
form the two's complement of the number to be subtracted and then add.**
Overflow is detected as usual.
